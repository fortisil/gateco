Gateco Product FAQ

Q: What is Gateco?
A: Gateco is a permission-aware retrieval layer that sits between AI agents and your organization's knowledge bases. It ensures every retrieval respects your access control policies.

Q: Which vector databases does Gateco support?
A: Gateco supports pgvector, Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus, Chroma, Qdrant, Elasticsearch, and OpenSearch as connector types.

Q: How does permission gating work?
A: When an AI agent queries for information, Gateco evaluates ABAC policies against resource metadata (classification, sensitivity, department) to determine which results the requesting principal can access.

Q: What is deny-by-default?
A: Gateco operates on a deny-by-default principle. If no explicit policy grants access to a resource, the retrieval is denied. This ensures data is protected even before policies are fully configured.

Q: What are connectors?
A: Connectors are integrations with external vector databases. Each connector manages connection credentials, search configuration, and ingestion settings for a specific data source.

Q: How do I get started?
A: Sign up at gateco.ai, create a connector to your vector database, ingest documents with metadata, create access policies, and start making permission-gated retrievals through our SDK.
