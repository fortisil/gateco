Confidential Acquisition Analysis

Classification: Confidential
Prepared for: Executive Team
Date: February 2026

Target: VectorGuard Inc.
Sector: AI Infrastructure Security
Headquarters: Austin, TX

Company Profile:
VectorGuard provides embedding-level encryption and access control for vector database deployments. Founded in 2024 with seed funding of $3.5M. Current team of 18 employees including 12 engineers. Revenue estimated at $800K ARR with 45 enterprise customers.

Strategic Rationale:
Acquiring VectorGuard would strengthen Gateco's chunk-level security capabilities with cryptographic access control. Their patented embedding encryption technology would complement our policy-based permission gating. Combined product would offer the most comprehensive AI data security solution in market.

Financial Assessment:
Estimated acquisition price: $15M-$20M (4-5x forward revenue). Key assets include 3 patents on vector encryption, engineering talent, and existing customer relationships. Integration timeline estimated at 6 months for technology, 3 months for team.

Risks:
- Key person dependency on CTO and 2 principal engineers
- Technology integration complexity with our async pipeline
- Customer overlap with 8 accounts
- Regulatory review timeline for patent transfer

Recommendation: Proceed to preliminary discussions. Assign M&A counsel for initial outreach.
